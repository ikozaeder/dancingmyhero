"The theme is about nominating your body as the hero! To let our bodies speak, to give thanks to our life and to the medium(our body), that allows us to suffer and feel happy, to learn and to fall. We want to hear its reactions, observe the imagery, sensations, to express and unfold the invisible and lead us further on. The whole purpose of the project is to share this magic with the audience, to remind them how powerful, vulnerable and multiple the body can be, and how important is to listen to our bodies and to celebrate Life."
